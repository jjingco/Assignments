Relaxed

Color Strategy: Complementary...

I wanted to write something in every page to make it less generic and so here it goes: Relaxed to are colours that don't usually pop out on the page -they are more subtle, more simple and just overall easy to read. They shouldn't make your eyes too tired and I think most of that should be driven by the amount of contrast on the page making sure the lettering and the background isn't too harsh for the readers. Afterall, you do just want them to "relax..."
